Imam (Dr). Idris Oni, born in the 80s to the family of Alhaji and Alhaja Abdur-Rasaq Oni, had his pre-primary education between 1992-1998 during which he had also started his Arabic and Islamic training at Markaz Da'wah al-Islamiyyah under the wings of the late polyglot, Ustadh AbdulWaheed Jumu-ah (RA).

He had his secondary education at Stadium Grammar School, Ifako-Ijaiye and concluded it at Sango Senior Secondary School, Agege in 2004.

He read Islamic Studies at the prestigious premium university of education in Nigeria, Tai Solarin University of Education where he bagged First Class (HONs) and graduated as the best graduating student in the College of Humanities (COHUM) in 2011.

He taught as a youth corp teacher at Kano's Tahfīzul-Qur'ān, Kibiya before his redeployment to Oyo State where he concluded his youth corp service at Mufu Lanihun High School.

Dr. Oni started and concluded his Master's Degree in Islamic Studies focusing on Muslim Societies at Olabisi Onabanjo University, Ago-Iwoye, Ogun State between 2013-2015. From the same university, he completed his PhD research specializing in Islamic Eschatology, a branch of Islamic Theology. He is currently a Ratībi Imām at Ar-Rahīm Mosque, Laderin Abeokuta and Acting Chief Imām at Taqwā Mosque, Lafenwa, Abeokuta. Dr. Idris Oni is also a Senior Lecturer and Head of Department of Islamic Studies at Crescent University, Abeokuta.

He is married and blessed with children.
