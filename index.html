About Imam (Dr). Idris

Brief Citation of Imam Idris A. Oni (Ph.D.)

Imam Idris A. Oni (Ph.D.) read Islamic Studies at the prestigious premium university of education in Nigeria, Tai Solarin University of Education where he bagged First Class (HONs) and graduated as the best graduating student in the College of Humanities (COHUM) in 2011.

Dr. Oni started and concluded his Master's Degree in Islamic Studies focusing on Muslim Societies at Olabisi Onabanjo University, Ago-Iwoye, Ogun State between 2013-2015.
